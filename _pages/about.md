Since June 2025, I am postdoc at the CEA Paris-Saclay (LSMA), Inria Saclay and Ecole Polytechnique (<a href="https://cmap.ip-paris.fr/recherche/analyse/ananke">ANENKE</a>), under the supervision of <a href="https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Alexandre-Imperiale-2">Alexandre Imperiale.</a>  and 
<a href="http://www.cmap.polytechnique.fr/~philippe.moireau/">Philippe Moireau.</a>. We are working on an inverse problem to estimate effective coefficients in asymptotic elastodynamics with non-periodic roughness, in particular, we focus on Kalman Filter methods.
I earned my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics in March 2025, in a cotutelle between the Universidad de Chile (<a href="https://www.dim.uchile.cl/">DIM</a>) and the Université de Pau et des Pays de l'Adour (<a href="https://lma-umr5142.univ-pau.fr/en/home.html">LMAP</a>), under the supervision of <a href="https://carlosconca.cl/en/inicio-english/">Carlos Conca</a>, <a href="https://fcaubet001.perso.univ-pau.fr/">Fabien Caubet</a> and <a href="https://mdambrin.perso.univ-pau.fr/">Marc Dambrine</a>.
I did my engineering and master studies at the Universidad de Chili, which I finished in 2019 and 2020, respectively. In particular, my master thesis was done under the supervision of <a href="https://carlosconca.cl/en/inicio-english/">Carlos Conca</a> and <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=dfnRrZkAAAAJ&hl=en">Jorge San Martin</a>.

My main research interests are:
======
- Shape optimization.
- Inverse problems.
- Numerical analysis.
- Scientific computing.

I aim to address real-world engineering and physical problems, such as fluid dynamics and wave propagation, by spanning the whole process: from mathematical modeling and rigorus analysis to proposing and implementing robusit, high-performance numerical methods.
